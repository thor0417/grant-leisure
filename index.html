Who We Are

Four decades of unmatched industry authority

Grant Leisure is a global leader in the strategic planning and implementation of visitor attractions and resort destinations. With offices in the United States and Europe, our team of accomplished senior consultants brings proven track records to every engagement.

Over 150 million people annually pay admission to the heritage sites, museums, visitor attractions, and theme parks around the world that Grant Leisure has consulted, owned, and operated.
