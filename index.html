Why Grant Leisure?

The development of a new attraction is a lofty venture

Over 150 million people pay admissions to the heritage sites, museums, visitor attractions, zoological institutions, and theme parks around the world that Grant Leisure has consulted, owned, and operated.

Because of the significant capital required, it is critically important that these projects are well designed, planned, and evaluated with economic certainty.

90%
of operational failures are a result of improper planning
Before you commit — you need answers to:
What trends are developing which might bring new forms of leisure into demand?
Where should a proposed attraction or resort be built?
What will the potential market be next year? In ten years? In twenty?
What are the capital requirements, financing options, and cash flow needs?
What will be your gross and net income?
How many visitors will there be and how will they arrive?
What other facilities are needed to entertain visitors and keep their interest?
