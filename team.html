The Team

50+ YRS · UNIVERSAL TO ENGLISH HERITAGE

Andy Grant

Chief Executive Officer

Andy's 50+ year career began at Universal Studios Hollywood, where he holds claim to being one of the park's first-ever studio tour guides. After climbing the ranks of Universal Studios to senior management, Andy went on to become the managing director for Busch Gardens, Squaw Valley Ski Resort, and the San Diego Zoo & Safari Park — and spent 12 years in charge of Leeds Castle in the United Kingdom. It was during Andy's tenure in London that Grant Leisure was founded and grew to become the foremost consultancy for the English Heritage and a globally recognized operator for the themed entertainment industry.

35 YRS · TEN COUNTRIES · FERRARI WORLD

Claus Frimand

Director Operations

Claus brings 35 years of experience in the service and leisure industry and has been an expat for over 25 years. He has lived in ten different countries working in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia on many high profile projects and was responsible for opening Ferrari World in Abu Dhabi. Claus's breadth of expertise and insight for recruitment and operations has been an asset to Grant Leisure, having worked for organizations such as IKEA, Disneyland Paris, the Olympics, EXPO 2000, Expo.02, and several international traveling exhibitions.

FOUR SEASONS · ASPEN · HK · US NPS

Clive Jones

Director Strategic Planning

Clive has evaluated investment programs and solicited investors and operators for major hotels, resorts, and casinos throughout Asia-Pacific, the Americas, and Europe. His expertise in market and investment analysis, development programming, and database marketing has earned him a sterling reputation within the attractions, hospitality, and tourism industries — having provided the economic planning for resorts such as Four Seasons Hualalai, Aspen Snowmass, Northstar Lake Tahoe, and Marriott Vacation Club, among many others. Complementing his private sector experience, Clive has also consulted to local, state and national tourism and recreation organizations. Notable clients include the US National Park Service, Hong Kong Tourism Board, Canadian Tourism Board, the state of California, and the city of San Francisco.

40 YRS · THEME PARKS · ENGINEERING

Keith Robertson

Director Rides, Shows & Attractions

Keith is a well-rounded senior executive with over 40 years of international project management experience in design, engineering, and operations for the development of major electrical power systems, commercial, industrial and residential construction, theme parks, water parks, tourism, and hospitality. His high-energy approach and diversified experience in strategic planning, training, staffing, maintenance, and operations has proven invaluable for his clients as he continues driving innovative engineering and management solutions.

DISNEY · THEMED ENTERTAINMENT

Robert Liljenwall

SVP Marketing & Branding

Robert has been a long-time principal of Grant Leisure — serving as head of its marketing and branding services and providing initial concept and creative direction for a broad spectrum of leisure attractions, visitor services, and integrated marketing communications programs. His career as a themed entertainment industry executive began with Disney and he has since worked on leisure and entertainment projects spanning a variety of themed attractions, zoological parks, resorts, film studios, themed entertainment centers, and new urban developments. Robert is an expert with developing a project's customer marketing matrix, identifying how best to serve visitors, maximize revenue streams, and ensure the highest degree of customer satisfaction.

FERRARI WORLD · NBC UNIVERSAL · BBC

Raul Rios

Director Consulting Operations, Europe

Raul brings over 15 years of industry experience and manages the consulting back-office for Grant Leisure's operations outside the US. Initially acting as Director of Projects and Commercial Controller, he was later appointed as Director for an international marketing services group. As MD of the German subsidiaries he was responsible for their successful restructuring, merger and change process. Working as a consultant, his input ranges from preparing financial feasibilities, business and operational plans, and overseeing attraction construction projects for a number of international clients including Olympic Park Legacy Company, Ferrari World Abu Dhabi, Carlsberg, NBC Universal (UK), Sylt Aquarium, Westfield and BBC.

ISO IWA 37 · UL CANADA

Philip is a compliance and operations consultant with eight years of experience in highly regulated and emerging industries.
