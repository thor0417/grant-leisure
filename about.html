London · 1980

The Tourist.

The year was 1980. In the wee small hours of a stormy London night, Andy Grant awoke from a dream and sketched a vision from his mind’s eye — a vision that would become the foundation of Grant Leisure and its principles.

An innovative concept — especially for the time. Embracing an experiential approach to developing attractions, focused on immersing visitors in the experience by ensuring all of their needs are met.

The Tourist has been and always will be Grant Leisure’s primary consideration for all of our projects.
