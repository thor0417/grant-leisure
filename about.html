Who We Are

About Grant Leisure

A global consultancy built on four decades of hands-on experience in the planning, development, and operation of world-class leisure destinations.

Our Story

The authority behind landmark leisure

Grant Leisure is a global leader in the strategic planning and implementation of visitor attractions and resort destinations. With offices in the United States and Europe, our team of accomplished senior consultants brings proven track records across every category of leisure development.

Over 150 million people annually pay admission to the heritage sites, museums, visitor attractions, zoological institutions, and theme parks around the world that Grant Leisure has consulted, owned, and operated.

"90% of operational failures are a result of improper planning. We exist to change that statistic."

Before committing significant capital to competitive, sophisticated markets, our clients deserve certainty. Grant Leisure delivers innovative solutions paired with realistic guidance throughout the entire planning and development process — at any stage, at any scale.
