REF_01
Market Analysis

Understanding the opportunity before a decision is made.

Grant Leisure assesses market support by evaluating overall market trends and growth in targeted markets. Consumer surveys, focus groups, and other market research techniques are used to test and refine demand estimates and market profiles to ensure long-term viability.

REF_02
Development Planning

Where economic reality and physical vision meet.

Grant Leisure works directly with architects, planners, engineers, and other professionals to achieve the optimum balance between economic planning and physical design. This results in creative development programmes that are responsive to the market and financially viable from the ground up.

REF_03
Financial Feasibility

Proprietary models. No guesswork.

Grant Leisure has created proprietary financial models for preparing cash flow and income projections, determining financial rates of return, and sensitivity testing of multi-use community development. These tools allow for rigorous audit of income properties and portfolio disposition programmes.

REF_04
Funding Assistance

Capital identified, evaluated, secured.

Grant Leisure identifies and evaluates acquisition and investment opportunities for our clients and provides assistance in purchase, sale, lease, and financing transaction negotiations. We reach out to our own global network of investors and financiers to evaluate interest and facilitate strategic introductions.

REF_05
Operational Planning

Every system. Every team. Every process.

Grant Leisure reviews performance, examines areas for expansion and revitalisation, and prepares pricing strategies and marketing programmes. We assist in operator selection and tenant negotiation, evaluating financial restructuring and disposition alternatives to maximize efficiency.

REF_06
Turnkey Management

We run it. Then we hand it over.

Clients profit from Grant Leisure’s decades of operating experience applied to the policies and procedures that become the operational foundation for their venture. Our team members monitor operations and make adjustments until the client is left with a fully self-sufficient and profitable operation.
