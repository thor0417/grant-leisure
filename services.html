What We Do

Full-spectrum expertise
at every stage

From initial market analysis through to operational launch and ongoing management — Grant Leisure delivers at every phase of the development lifecycle.

01

Market Analysis

Assessing market support by evaluating overall trends and growth in target markets. We answer the questions that determine whether a project has a real, sustainable audience.

  • Visitor demand forecasting
  • Competitive landscape evaluation
  • Tourism growth analysis
  • Demographics & catchment mapping
02

Concept Development

Building compelling, differentiated development concepts based on identified target markets, competitive supply, location attributes, and brand positioning.

  • Attraction concept design
  • Brand & theming strategy
  • Programme & mix planning
  • Site evaluation & selection
03

Feasibility Studies

Rigorous financial feasibility assessments, operating pro formas, and cash flow projections to establish economic certainty before significant capital is committed.

  • Financial modelling & pro formas
  • Capital cost estimates
  • Cash flow projections
  • Sensitivity analysis
04

Funding Assistance

Strategic assistance with soliciting and securing funding opportunities, working in conjunction with our financial partners to structure optimal deal frameworks for each project.

  • Investor presentation preparation
  • Funding source identification
  • Deal structure advisory
  • Partner introductions
05

Operational Planning

Comprehensive operational strategy, performance reviews, and targeted recommendations for improvement and expansion — from first opening through ongoing optimisation.

  • Operations manuals & SOPs
  • Staffing structures & org charts
  • Revenue optimisation
  • Performance benchmarking
06

Turn-Key Management

Executive staff recruitment and full turn-key operational management from pre-opening through Grand Opening and post-launch, ensuring your destination launches successfully.

  • Executive recruitment
  • Pre-opening management
  • Grand Opening planning
  • Ongoing management advisory
How We Work

A proven framework for certainty

Grant Leisure can supply the necessary resources at any stage of a project's development and management.

01
Site & Market Evaluation

Where should it be built? Who is the market next year, in ten years, in twenty? We establish the full picture before a single development decision is made.

02
Concept Validation

What trends are developing? What forms of leisure will come into demand? We match your concept to real, demonstrable opportunity in the target market.

03
Capital Requirements & Feasibility

Gross and net income projections, financing options, cash flow needs — full economic certainty before commitment of significant capital resources.

04
Development Planning

Hotel facilities, ancillary services, visitor flow, infrastructure — every element planned to keep visitor interest and drive per-capita spend.

05
Funding Structure

We work alongside financial partners to secure the optimal funding architecture for leisure and resort developments of every scale and geography.

06
Operational Launch & Beyond

From pre-opening staffing to Grand Opening day — and the ongoing management that follows — Grant Leisure remains your strategic partner.
