Our Approach

Our goal is to create self-sustaining development ecosystems that capitalise existing assets and encourage expansion. Resulting in longer visitor length of stay and increased profits.

01
Market Analysis

Understanding the opportunity before a single decision is made.

Grant Leisure assesses market support by evaluating overall market trends and growth in targeted markets. Consumer surveys, focus groups, and other market research techniques are used to test and refine demand estimates and market profiles.

02
Development Planning

Where economic reality and physical vision meet.

Grant Leisure works directly with architects, planners, engineers, and other professionals to achieve the optimum balance between economic planning and physical design, resulting in real estate products and creative development programs that are responsive to the market and financially viable.

03
Financial Feasibility

Proprietary models. No guesswork.

Grant Leisure has created proprietary financial models for preparing cash flow and income projections, determining financial rates of return, and sensitivity testing of multi-use community development, income properties, and portfolio disposition programs.

From concept to operation.
40 years. Every continent.

04
Funding Assistance

Capital identified, evaluated, secured.

Grant Leisure identifies and evaluates acquisition and investment opportunities for our clients and provides assistance in purchase, sale, lease, and financing transaction negotiations. We additionally reach out to our own network of investors and financiers to evaluate interest and make introductions.

05
Operational Planning

Every system. Every team. Every process.

Grant Leisure reviews performance, examines areas for expansion and revitalisation, prepares pricing strategies and marketing programs, assists in operator selection and tenant negotiation, and evaluates financial restructuring and disposition alternatives.

06
Turnkey Management

We run it. Then we hand it over.

Clients profit from Grant Leisure's decades of operating experience applied to the policies and procedures that become the operational foundation for their venture. Team members are reserved for two years following opening to monitor operations and make adjustments until the client is left with a self-sufficient operation.
